Rustic Design By Leigha, is a custom design brand that specializes in making state of the art wooden decor and furniture. The brand is both founded and ran by Leigha Torney. The company first started in 2016 out of her parents garage in Western Shawnee, KS. Leigha has continued to grow her brand to the size it is today and has expanded her abilities in both sign and furniture craftsmanship. Leigha is currently in the process of completing the Interior Design program at a local community college. Alongwith continuing to expand rustic design, she hopes to become a licensed interior designer with hope of remodeling homes in the near future!

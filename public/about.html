Who are we?

Rustic Design By Leigha is a small business that makes unique and completely handcrafted furniture and wood designs. Rustic Design started when the founder Leigha Torney was in 8th grade. She began to work with wood in her parents Garage in Shawnee Ks, and has continued to grow the business since those first days. Leigha originally began Rustic Design by selling simple blanket ladders to friends and family. She then began to expand her knowledge and skills and started to create wooden decor, such as signs and ornaments. Rustic Design, takes custom orders for signs and will work with the costumer to create a unique, and state of the art project as well.

What we do?

Rustic Design is the place to shop when it comes to custom wooden projects or decoration. The signs that are made can be customized to each customers name and information. Rustic Design also creates personalized projects that can range from sliding barn doors, coffee table kennels, end tables, coffee tables, and more! If you dream it then Rustic Design can make it.
